Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Is the website a streaming platform?

This website uses The Movie Database to search for movies and series, providing details. However, the actual streaming is facilitated through a third-party server.

2. How does the Auto Update feature work?

Videos are automatically updated with new or better quality as soon as they become available online.

3. Are videos protected from DMCA?

Yes, our videos are secure and protected to prevent removal due to DMCA notices.

4. Can I watch any movie or TV series I search for?

Please note that all search results may not be available for streaming. If the video is available on the server, you can watch it. However, there are over 50,000 movies available, and the collection keeps updating every minute.
